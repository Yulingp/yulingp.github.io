About the Artist, Yuling Peng (彭郁舲)

My life is my canvas. I paint dreams!

Before 2021, most people knew me for my "intellectual" work (outlined below), but imagination and creativity are the key to my happiness.

I drew a lot as a kid, making birthday and holiday cards for my parents each year and doodling in my textbooks. In 2011, I self-published an illustration book about my student exchange life in Lyon, France.

I was born in the US, grew up in Taiwan, and have lived in Canada, France, Spain, Germany, and Estonia. These diverse experiences have deeply influenced my art and how I view people and the world.

My Creative Process

I draw emotions and states of mind inspired by real-life experiences. Life events, people, and objects can all be my inspiration.

Oftentimes, a vivid-yet-rough visual would form in my mind, then I'd try to capture that vision on paper and intuitively choose colors, details, and complementary objects as I go.
